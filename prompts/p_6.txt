### Fields you MUST fill

**`new_world_facts`**
  List of new, immutable facts about the world that this episode establishes.
  Draw only from explicit dialogue and action text in Script.json — do not infer
  or embellish. A fact is immutable if it cannot be undone by later episodes
  (e.g. a death, a location revealed, an artefact named, a law declared).
  Format: array of concise declarative strings.
  Use `[]` if the episode establishes no new world facts.

**`new_unresolved_threads`**
  List of open plot questions or cliffhangers this episode introduces.
  Each thread must be a question or incomplete situation that the audience is
  left wondering about. Do not include threads that are resolved within the same
  episode. Do not duplicate threads already present in `canon.json`.
  Format: array of concise strings phrased as open questions or dangling states.
  Use `[]` if no new threads are opened.

**`resolved_threads`**
  List of threads from `canon.unresolved_threads` (if canon.json exists) that
  are definitively closed by this episode's events or revelations.
  Reference threads by their exact text as it appears in canon.json.
  Use `[]` if no prior threads are resolved, or if canon.json does not exist.

### Rules

  - Do NOT contradict anything in canon.json.
  - Do NOT invent facts or threads not evidenced in Script.json dialogue.
  - Do NOT leave any `__FILL__` markers in the output.
