### Fields you MUST fill (marked `__FILL__` in the scaffold)

**`background_id`**
  Format: `"bg-<descriptive-slug>"` (e.g. `"bg-nile-at-dusk"`, `"bg-burial-chamber-torchlight"`)
  Derive the slug from the VO text mood and implied setting of the shot.
  Multiple shots may share the same `background_id` when the scene does not change.

**`camera_framing`**
  Choose exactly one:
    `"close-up"` — tight on a detail or face; emotional intensity
    `"medium"`   — balanced; most common for dialogue or narrative beats
    `"wide"`     — reveals environment; used on scene openings or establishing moments
    `"extreme-wide"` — epic scale; landscape, crowd, or geography shots

**`camera_movement`**
  Choose exactly one:
    `"static"`    — locked-off; stability, contemplation
    `"slow_push"` — gradual zoom-in; building tension or intimacy
    `"tracking"`  — follows subject or pans through space
    `"pan"`       — horizontal sweep; reveal or survey
    `"tilt"`      — vertical sweep; scale or ascent/descent

**`audio_intent.sfx_tags`**
  Array of 1–3 short strings. Name the physical sound, not the emotion.
  Examples: `"wind_gentle"`, `"footsteps_stone"`, `"crowd_murmur"`, `"fire_crackling"`
  Use `[]` only when the shot is genuinely silent (rare).

**`audio_intent.music_mood`**
  One concise phrase describing the music direction, e.g. `"tense strings, low drone"`.
  Set to `null` only when no underscoring is needed (e.g. an isolated SFX moment).
